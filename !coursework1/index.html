What is the plot?

Widley considered the first 'zombie flick' that follows the modern zombie 'stereotypes', Night of the Living Dead is a film where people must survive the zombie outbreak. After visting a cemetery to pay respects, fJohnny and Barbara (Judith O'Dea) are suddenly confronted by...the UNDEAD! During the commotion Barbara and Johnny split up. Wandering alone Barbara stumbles accross a house where other survivors are held up. Among them are 'Ben' (Duane Jones), 'Harry Cooper' (Karl Hardman) and 'Helen Cooper' (Marilyn Eastman). They must survive the outbreak but what will come of them?
